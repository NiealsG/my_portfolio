A bit about me

I’ve always been passionate about design, though it did take me a while to find my way to UX/UI. Back in the day, I was the curly-haired kid you’d find in the playground when you needed some answers. I’m all about finding solutions and simplifying life. And if those solutions look pretty too, well that’s just a bonus. I’m fluent in Italian (and can manage some basic French & Spanish), and always looking to practice my speaking skills over coffee. If you challenge me to a Friends quiz, I will win. South African born and bred, I’m currently all about that remote-worker lifestyle and travelling as much as possible.
